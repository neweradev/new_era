THE NEW ERA

The new is coming!
Open international profight league is coming live! Stay tuned for new series of ranked profight events which will start in Strzelce Krajeńskie. Registration, ruleset, and event plan will soon be available for all fighters in the Base and Pro group. The New Era will create the opportunity to fight the best fighters and for those who want to start and see if the profight category is for them to try their skills!
